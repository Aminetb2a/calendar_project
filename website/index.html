Our Features

Calendar

An Interactive Calendar is Implemented to display the Items in the Timiline, Tasks,Events, and Meetings.

Tasks

The Ability of creating Tasks, Display them in the Calendar timeline, Assign Tasks to specific Users of your Application.

Events

Add your Special Events to your Database, display them and let your Employees be in touch with the upcoming Events.

Meetings

Don't waste time informing your Users about the Meeting, When? who's in?..., just Add it to the timeline and they will receive Notifications..!.

Statistics

Nice and Beatiful Charts design has been used in this application, to let the Admins well updated about the Progress of Tasks.

Responsive Design

Nice and Beautiful UI design is used, using the last web application trends.

About

Here's an Introduction to our Software

purposes of our project we needed a simple utility to schedule tasks. Holidays and meeting, give the users more creative space of interaction between workers, workers and leaders, and between teams for such a big companies. Our applications will have a new, smooth and creative user interface design, in which the users are able to feel relax and comfortable, in addition to that it will contain a chat box in the bottom as what the social networks have, where the user can stay connected with their leaders and other members, or they will have a team conversation so there will be a connectivity between the members.
So when the user log in the application will recognize its position whether the user is a manger so he/she is going to log in into a special control panel which is special for the leaders and managers, and for the managers or administrator level will view graphs of the backlog development, then they will find a panel in which they will view how many workers are available and who submitted and finished his duties. But for the workers will receive just notifications and duties or tasks that it should be done within the required timing, and they can submit their feedback too.
